What is this site?


whataretheylisteningto.com is an album-centric music tracking site.
Akin to what you might do with tracks, you can organize Albums into listening series(ex. Summer 2021), or lists(Top Hip-Hop Albums of 2021).
In addition, you can leave reviews of albums, and keep a backlog with a public suggestion form.

This site was originally created by Joey Martin as whatsjoeylisteningto.com, a site that allowed him to share and organize all of the album he has listened to. With the help and suggestions of fellow music fans, this service went public and now exists as it does today.
